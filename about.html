Our Story

Premier Roofing Co. was founded in 2003 with a simple mission: to provide exceptional roofing services with integrity, quality, and care. What started as a small family business has grown into one of the region's most trusted roofing companies.

Our founder, with over 30 years of experience in the roofing industry, established the company on the principles of honest work, fair pricing, and treating every home as if it were our own. These values continue to guide everything we do today.

Over the years, we've completed more than 500 projects, from simple repairs to complex commercial installations. Our success comes from our commitment to quality craftsmanship and our dedication to customer satisfaction.
