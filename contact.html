Frequently Asked Questions

Quick answers to common questions

How long does a roof replacement take?

Most residential roof replacements are completed in 1-3 days, depending on the size and complexity of the project.

Do you offer financing?

Yes, we offer flexible financing options to help make your roofing project affordable. Ask us for details.

What warranties do you provide?

We offer manufacturer warranties on materials plus our own workmanship warranty. Details vary by product.

Do you work with insurance?

Yes, we regularly work with insurance companies and can help you navigate the claims process for storm damage.
